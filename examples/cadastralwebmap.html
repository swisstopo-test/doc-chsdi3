Cadastralwebmap as WMTS

WMTS

This examples shows how to use geo.admin.ch CadastralWebMap WMTS layers. The WMTS tiles are generated on-the-fly by a MapProxy service and hold in cache for one hour. The only value for the timestamp dimension is current. Not other values are possible as outlined in the WMTS GetCapabilities.
